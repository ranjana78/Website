About Myself

Hi, I am a Phd Scholar at IIT Ropar and my current research topic and area is exploring efficient few shot learning for medical image analysis. I am also very much interested and open to works on machine learning or deep learning applications on medical image processing or analysis. Interested to work with enthusiastic research groups and labs and am open for collaboration. I did my M.Tech from IIIT Guwahati where my research domain was on Services Computing, QoS Prediction  and QoS Composition. I completed my B.Tech in CSE from Assam University Silchar. I am currently pursuing my PhD under Dr. Deepti R. Bathula (Associate Professor at CSE, IIT Ropar). For knowing more about my publications please scroll below.
